Italian Dressing

Description

Want a fast and delicious Italian dressing you can make at home? You’re going to love this! Just shake everything together in a jar. That’s it! No cooking, no chopping, no blending, no nothing.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

  1. Purchase all the ingredients from the store.
  2. Add all ingredients to a jar, place the lid on the jar, and give it a good shake!
  3. Just like with all vinegar based dressing you'll need to shake it up again before pouring to eliminate separation.
